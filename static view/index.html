    What is Phanet?

    Phanerosis Prayer Network International/Phanerosis Network (PHANET) is a fast expanding group of intercessors of all ages and sexes. The network is a not-for-profit, non-governmental, interdenominational network of Christians, with the passion to intercede and share the word of God with the world.

    We believe in God the father almighty, creator of heaven and earth, and in his ability to manifest his power through the prayers and intercessions of men and women; to reach the unreached, strengthen the weak, support the strong, fellowship with his church, and enlighten his people through the study of the word of God, that all may come to the saving knowledge of the Lord Jesus Christ.
